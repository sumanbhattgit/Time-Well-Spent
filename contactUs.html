Featured Story

Helen

Charity: Highams Park Food Aid

I volunteer to feel part of the community, and to spend my time doing mething fun and valuable with so like-minded people. I’m also gaining work experience while looking for a job. I graduated in July and I was thinking about volunteering when I saw a call for volunteers on Highams Park Food Aid’s Facebook page. I was immediately welcomed on to the team, and was given more and more projects to get involved with as time went on. I am now the administrator for the Hub. I register and coordinate the volunteers, greet the beneficiaries and help to organise food storage systems.

Peter

Charity: Newham Welfare Trust

Volunteering has enabled me to use my knowledge and experience much more widely than would have been possible otherwise. It has given me friendships, contacts and experiences that have enriched my life. I volunteer to give back to my community, and to spend my time doing something valuable. Volunteering gives me a valuable way to spend my time and a positive outlook on life. It keeps me grounded and moving forward during an incredibly uncertain time.
