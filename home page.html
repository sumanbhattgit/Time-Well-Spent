Opportunities

There are more than 185,000 charities in the UK, hundreds of which are in London.

We want to connect people with spare time and who feel isolated
from working at home with their local community through volunteering.
There are dozens of types of charities with roles for every type of person.
You can use this website to find out about your closest charities and how
to get involved. It's a challenging time for everyone at the moment due to
Coronavirus. It is more important than ever to support and help out local community.

Enjoyable

Volunteering allows you to spend time with like minded people from different backgrounds and with different interests. Even during lockdown!

Employable

Spending your free time volunteering gives you ways to fill gaps in your CV, while also allowing you to learn valuable transferable skills for all kinds of environments.

Meaningful

Many volunteers give time in their busy, existing work schedules because they care about the cause and want to spend their time doing a good thing. Volunteering isn’t just for those with hours and hours to spare!
